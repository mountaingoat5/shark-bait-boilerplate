A Day in the Life of...
A DOPE BACKPACKING TRIP

The Crew: Myself Aaron, and Mountain Man Tej

Fun fact: the Sand dunes were created from the left over
sandy deposits from when the San Juan Valley was a
giant ocean. The Sand was then blown into this corner of the valley
up against the Sangre de Chrito Mountain Range.

In a day in the life of a dope backpacking trip,
we pitch out tents in super cool forests.
There's only one thing to do at a time like this:
take a hike.

After our Hike, we need to head back and find a new spot for the tent!
This time, we wanted soft molding sand for our backs,
so we found the best patch of sand and saddled up!

This is our new home for the night!
We slept the worst here, but made it through by making sure we were all awake together
to ward off the wolves playing on the dunes
